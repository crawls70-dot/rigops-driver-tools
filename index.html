HOS REGULATIONS — QUICK REFERENCEFMCSA
11-Hour Rule
Max 11 hrs driving after 10 consecutive hrs off duty
14-Hour Rule
Cannot drive beyond 14th consecutive hr after coming on duty
30-Minute Break
Must take 30-min break after 8 hrs of driving without a break
10-Hour Off Duty
Must be off duty for at least 10 consecutive hours between shifts
60/7 Day Limit
Cannot drive after 60 hrs on-duty in 7 consecutive days
70/8 Day Limit
Cannot drive after 70 hrs on-duty in 8 consecutive days
34-Hour Restart
Can restart 7/8-day period after 34 consecutive hrs off-duty
Sleeper Berth Split
